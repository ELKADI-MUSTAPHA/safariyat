OUR SERVICES

BIRTHDAY/SPECIAL EVENT

You want to organize an unforgettable and unusual birthday, wedding anniversary, bachelor(ette) party, or a special event ? We will find you the trip to best suit your desires and your pocket.

CORPORATE AWAY DAYS

We organize seminars from 20 to 200 people, you can personalize one of our unusual tours and ask for extra services. We offer many activities: rallies, guided tours, gala events, water sports, trail, dromedary rides...

TRAVEL INSURANCE

Each traveller must have a personal travel insurance -(repatriation and cancellation insurance) We advise you to get in touch with our French insurance company partner Chapka Assurances.

EQUIPMENT RENTAL

Sleeping bag, hiking bag, poles, bikes, mountaineering equipment, tents ... We can rent you all the equipment needed that you do not want to take on a trip.

VILLA AND RIAD RENTAL

You want to get an exceptional accommodation that is worth 1001 nights just for you? In Marrakech, Essaouira, Tangier, Fez ... We find your villa or riad depending on your budget! And we take care of everything else

Guided/Escorted Tour Vacation

Exploring a new destination on your vacation for the first time is an exhilarating experience. there are many guided/escorted tours to enjoy while on vacation. We make sure you don’t miss out on anything!
